About Us

Dogu Yildizi A.S. was founded in 2020 in Şişli, Istanbul. Our company specializes in importing, sourcing, and wholesaling machinery and technical parts for industrial use.

We work with long-term partners from Europe to procure quality products, which we process through our logistics network in Turkey and deliver primarily to customers in the Middle East, especially Dubai.

We position Turkey as a strategic trade hub between Europe and the Middle East.

Our Strengths

  • Active leadership and hands-on business management
  • Long-standing partnerships with European and Asian suppliers
  • Professional support for customs and accounting
  • Diverse, high-quality product portfolio
  • Net profit (2024): 398,120.58 TL
