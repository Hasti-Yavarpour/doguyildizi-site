About Doğu Yıldızı

Founded in 2020 and headquartered in Şişli, Istanbul, Doğu Yıldızı A.Ş. is a rapidly growing multi-disciplinary company engaged in international and domestic trade. We specialize in the import, supply, and wholesale of industrial machinery, spare parts, and technical equipment, primarily serving the heavy industry sector.

Our operations bridge Europe, Turkey, and the Middle East, forming a reliable logistics and trade corridor. We source high-quality components from trusted European manufacturers and deliver them efficiently through our Turkish logistics infrastructure to clients, particularly in the Gulf region including Dubai.

We are proud of our long-term collaborations with reputable manufacturers and distributors across Europe and Asia. With an expanding product portfolio, robust financial management, and strong operational infrastructure, Doğu Yıldızı continues to grow steadily while remaining committed to quality, transparency, and strategic partnerships.
