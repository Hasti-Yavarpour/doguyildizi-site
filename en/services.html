Our Services

At Dogu Yildizi A.S., we specialize in the import, supply, and wholesale of heavy industrial equipment. Below are our main product groups:

  • 1. Industrial Gear and Motor Systems
    SK90–130 series geared motors, 5.5 kW – 15 kW high torque.
  • 2. Frequency Inverters
  • 3. Rolling Mill Equipment
    Hydro Couplings, Torque Transfer Units, Special Gear Components.
  • 4. Custom Industrial Machinery & Parts
    TACHO GENERATOR, VALVE SPRING, BIG END BOLT, etc.
  • 5. Spare Parts & Technical Components
    AIR FILTER, THERMOCOUPLE, RING, O-SEAL, PLUG

All products are of European origin and meet high-quality standards. Customer satisfaction and long-term supply are our priorities.
